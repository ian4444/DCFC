2015 - 2016 Team Captains

Tristan L. Chu '16

Prior to Dartmouth, I spent 2 years fencing foil for the Cardinal Fencing Club at Stanford University. I've been a part of DCFC for my entire time at Dartmouth; this team is like a family to me, and I can't stress how much its helped me grow and develop as both a person and a fencer.

I am both humbled and honored to serve as one of the co-captains for the 2015-2016 year, and I am excited to lead our team to our 3rd consecutive national title! When you join DCFC, you don't just join a team; you become part of a family with bonds that exist both on and off the strip. Welcome to our family, and I hope you choose to make DCFC a part of your Dartmouth experience.

Cecilia Lu '16

Hi! My name is Cecilia, and I am so excited to work with everyone this fall as we train for another great season!

A little bit about my fencing background: I started fencing épée in high school with Baltimore Fencing Club. Since then, I have fenced with DCFC throughout my college career. It's been an amazing experience, and I can't imagine what the past four years would have been like without this team. DCFC is a community that exists beyond just practices and competitions. It's here that you will find people to drag to the latest movie, cram for that exam, grab late night, or just talk about life. Welcome to Dartmouth, and I hope that you will join our DCFC family.
